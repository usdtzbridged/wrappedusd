How It Works

Wormhole follows a 'lock and mint' mechanism to perform secure cross-chain transactions.

1. Lock Tokens

Your USDT tokens are locked in a smart contract on the source blockchain network.

2. Cross-Chain Transfer

Wormhole's Guardian network validates the transaction and facilitates the cross-chain transfer.

3. Mint on Destination

Wrapped USDT tokens are minted on the destination chain, representing your locked tokens.
